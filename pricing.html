FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak season (Oct–Feb). Popular weekend dates can fill up quickly.

Is a deposit required to secure the date?

Yes. A 25% advance deposit is required to confirm your booking. The balance is due 30 days before the event.

Can I bring my own caterer?

Yes, external caterers are welcome. A kitchen usage fee applies. Our in-house catering is available at a discounted rate when bundled with hall booking.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations 90+ days before receive a 75% refund. Within 60 days — 50%. Within 30 days — no refund but date rescheduling is possible once.

Are prices negotiable?

Weekday and off-season bookings often have room for negotiation. Contact us to discuss a custom package within your budget.

Is there a separate charge for parking?

Basic parking is included in all packages. Valet parking service is available as an add-on.
