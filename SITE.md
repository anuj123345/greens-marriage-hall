# SITE.md — Greens Marriage Hall

## Site Name
**Greens**

## Tagline
*Where Your Dream Wedding Comes to Life*

## About the Site
Greens is a premium marriage hall website designed to help couples and families discover, explore, and book the perfect venue for their special day. The site conveys warmth, elegance, and trust — guiding visitors from inspiration to inquiry.

---

## Pages

### 1. Home
**Purpose:** The main landing page. Welcomes visitors with an emotional hero section featuring the hall's most beautiful imagery, a short headline about the experience Greens offers, and a clear call-to-action (Book a Tour / Enquire Now). Highlights key selling points — capacity, services, and location — in a scannable format. Includes a testimonial strip and a final CTA section.

### 2. Features
**Purpose:** Showcases everything Greens has to offer. Covers the hall's amenities (air conditioning, stage, lighting, sound system), capacity (indoor/outdoor seating), in-house catering, decoration packages, parking, bridal suite, and any additional services. Presented with icons, photos, and short descriptions to help families evaluate the venue.

### 3. Pricing
**Purpose:** Provides transparent, easy-to-compare pricing packages. Packages cover different event types (engagement ceremony, wedding reception, full-day booking) and guest counts. Each package lists what's included. Ends with a note encouraging custom quotes and a contact CTA.

### 4. About
**Purpose:** Tells the story of Greens — its history, the people behind it, and the values that drive the team. Builds trust and emotional connection with the audience. Includes a brief founder's note, team photos, milestones, and any awards or recognitions.

### 5. Contact
**Purpose:** The conversion page. Contains a booking enquiry form (name, date, event type, guest count, message), phone number, email, Google Maps embed for the venue location, and office hours. Makes it as easy as possible for interested visitors to reach out.

---

## Target Audience
Families and couples planning weddings, receptions, engagements, and other milestone celebrations.

## Visual Direction
Warm, playful, pastel color palette with rounded components. Soft greens, creams, and blush tones. Friendly typography with generous whitespace.
