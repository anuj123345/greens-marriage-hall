How It All Started

A Dream That Grew
Into a Legacy

Greens was born in 2008 from a very simple dream — to create a place where families could celebrate life's most meaningful moments without worry, without stress, and with complete joy.

What started as a modest hall near Mancheswar has grown into Bhubaneswar's most sought-after wedding venue. We expanded our garden terrace in 2014, added our luxury bridal suite in 2018, and completed our grand ballroom renovation in 2022 — each step guided by the feedback and trust of the families we serve.

Today, a team of 40+ passionate professionals keeps the Greens dream alive every single day — from the security guard who greets your guests at the gate to the senior coordinator who stays by your side from first enquiry to the last dance.
